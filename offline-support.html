Use the App Offline

Stay informed even when you're in remote areas with no network. AgroAid offers offline-first features that ensure uninterrupted access to essential resources.

  • Pre-download guides for crop care, pest control, and irrigation techniques
  • Access latest saved weather forecasts and alerts without internet
  • View previously fetched market prices and trends
  • Offline access to government schemes and subsidy info
  • Store personal farm records securely in browser cache
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) support for installable experience

Tip: Enable "Save for offline" in the app to download latest content before going offline.
