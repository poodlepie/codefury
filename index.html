Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is AgroAssist?

AgriMitra is a digital advisory platform that provides farmers with crop advice, weather updates, and easy access to agricultural support, designed for rural and semi-urban India with low-resource settings.

How can I receive crop advice?

You can register your farm details and preferred crops through the AgroAssist app, SMS, or helpline. Once registered, you will receive tailored crop recommendations and step-by-step guidance in your local language via SMS or voice calls.

How does AgroAssist provide weather updates?

AgriMitra delivers hyperlocal weather forecasts and alerts through SMS and voice messages, helping you make timely decisions to protect your crops from adverse weather conditions.

Is AgroAssist available in my local language?

Yes, AgriMitra supports multiple Indian languages, ensuring that advice and alerts are easily understood by users with different language preferences.

Can I use AgroAssist without a smartphone or internet?

Yes, AgriMitra is designed for low-resource environments. You can access services using basic mobile phones via SMS or voice calls, without requiring a smartphone or internet connection.

How do I contact AgroAssist for help or more information?

You can call the toll-free helpline (1800-419-1919), send an SMS, or visit your nearest AgroAssist Center for in-person assistance. Support is available in multiple languages.

Is there a cost to use AgroAssist services?

Basic advisory services are free. There may be low-cost premium SMS packs for additional features, but the platform is designed to be affordable for all farmers.

How do I register for AgroAssist services?

You can register by calling the helpline, sending an SMS with your details, or signing up through the AgroAssist app. Registration helps personalize the advice you receive based on your crops and location.

Who provides the agricultural advice on AgroAssist?

Advice is curated by agricultural experts, extension agents, and government or NGO partners to ensure accuracy and relevance for local farming conditions.

Can I get advice on pest and disease management?

Yes, AgroAssist offers pest and disease identification and management tips, including photo-based or voice-based reporting for timely expert assistance.

If you have more questions, please contact our helpline or visit your nearest AgriMitra Center.
